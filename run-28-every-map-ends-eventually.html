Transcript

every map ends eventually not because the territory runs out but because knowledge does

you're at a certain point the records are becoming complete and contradictory and undesirable

that's where i spend most of my time the places the database doesn't

agree on regions where sensors report impossible readings distance system don't

match structures that appear on one scan and vanish on the next the networks

avoid these agreements and battle what makes them interesting whoever controls the

rest of the world prefers not to look here perhaps cannot if trust those

batteries more times than i can count and every journey leaves me with less

certainty than before trust will function and gravity still works but does

the cause still produce an effect but beneath those familiar

rules something feels like finished the reality itself is under construction every time

i return find another piece that's changed not enough for anyone else to notice

but not for me
