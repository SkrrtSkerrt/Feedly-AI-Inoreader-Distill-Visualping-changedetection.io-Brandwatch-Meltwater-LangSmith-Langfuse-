Transcript

i found the photographs of the digital part of the natural photograph people

creased at the corners faded by decades of weather and neglect sure families

still know the photographs that go on exist instead of their homes not because

of who they were and their names or that's my name is the expression

of business i looked forward to their hesitation as if the future was something

predictable as if tomorrow was guaranteed but here the

tower stood transplants the sunlight still drift overhead and higher systems continue

operating long after the people who built the underground dust and nobody seems to

know why sometimes i think those shins are here to the earth and sometimes

i think they're no less than they know how to understand and something between

those possibilities is i keep walking searching for the reason everything is still running
