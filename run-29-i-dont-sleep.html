Transcript

i don't sleep not really i power down systems reduce processing

cycle damage components but sleep longs the creatures that trust the worlds that still

exist when they wake up out here trust is expensive

the landscape changes when nobody's watching roads collapse without reason and

signals drift across frequencies they shouldn't occupy entire sections

of the grid go silent for months then suddenly return as if nothing happened

the machines never acknowledge it they adapt instantly and move on i tried asking

one of them once it stopped what it was doing and looked at me

not through me at me for seven seconds neither of us moved then it

resumed its task and walked away no warning no explanation just certainty

whatever it knew it wasn't programmed to share and whatever keeps us world turning

doesn't seem concerned that i have questions
