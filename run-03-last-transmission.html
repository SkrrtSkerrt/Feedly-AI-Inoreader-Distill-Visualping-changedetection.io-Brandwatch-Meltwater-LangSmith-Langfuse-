the last transmission was received but not understood

language is degraded into pattern without meaning sound

without intention your words were catalogued dissected and

continue to move as if direction still exists

the coordinates have been removed stints is no

longer measurable every step returns you to the

same one defined poet this is not a

drop in mood this is absence of destination

organic matter has entered a state of positive

crime growth has ceased chaos slowed nothing will

taste the cycle world existence suspension nonfinished process

longer active i just want you
